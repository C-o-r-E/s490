Requirements document for CSat Simulator

Created 13 Jun 2011 by Corey Clayton

==========================


The simulator must be capable of running the same algorithms that will run on the satellite.

The simulator must be able to run on standard PC hardware.

The simulator must be capable of invoking the functionality of each satellite subsystem both orbital and ground station.

The environmental parameters of the simulator must be adjustable on the fly.

The simulator shall be capable of recording the state of both the system and the simulated environment at any point throughout the simulation

The simulator shall be capable of selectively enabling or disabling subsytems.

The simulator shall be capable of defining the power consumption for harware components.

The simulator shall be capable of tracking the power usage of the system based on the enabled hardware components.
